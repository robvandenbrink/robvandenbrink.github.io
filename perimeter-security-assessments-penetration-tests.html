Internet Perimeter Security

In a Perimeter Penetration test, we focus on your internet perimeter. This normally means internet-facing services. However, all too often organizations “forget” that the people inside your perimeter go home at night, that parts of your organization might be outside your perimeter defenses either some or all of the time. Let us help you define your “new perimeter”, and how you can defend all pieces of your infrastructure, both inside and outside your firewall. If you need us to assess your traditional perimeter, it’s strongly suggested that we do this as an assessment, and that we also include the internal networks and infrastructure.
